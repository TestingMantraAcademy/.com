Manual Testing Course

Discover the world of Manual Testing and equip yourself with the skills to rigorously assess software quality. Our Manual Testing Course is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of manual testing techniques, methodologies, and best practices.

What you'll learn

  • Testing Fundamentals:Learn the core principles of manual testing, including test planning, test case design, and execution.
  • Introduction to Manual Testing:Gain a foundational understanding of manual testing and its role in the software development life cycle.
  • Functional Testing Techniques:Explore functional testing methodologies and best practices for verifying that software applications meet their intended requirements.
  • Test Case Development:Master the art of creating effective test cases that cover various scenarios and validate software functionality.
  • Hands-On Experience: Gain practical experience by testing real-world software applications, allowing you to apply your knowledge in real scenarios.
  • Functional and Non-Functional Testing:Explore both functional and non-functional testing aspects, including usability, performance, and security testing.
